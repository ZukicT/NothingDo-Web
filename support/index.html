frequently asked

Is my data backed up anywhere?
No. Nothing Do is local-first. Your tasks, habits, and settings live only on your iPhone. There are no accounts and no cloud sync, so keep your device backed up via iCloud or a computer backup if you want a safety net.
I deleted the app. Can I recover my tasks?
Deleting the app removes its locally stored data. We cannot recover it because we never had a copy. Restoring a full device backup made before the deletion is the only way back.
Reminders aren't showing up. What should I check?
Make sure notifications are allowed for Nothing Do in iOS Settings → Notifications, and that Focus modes or Scheduled Summary aren't holding them back. Reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device.
Does Nothing Do track me or show ads?
No. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking of any kind. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.
Which devices are supported?
Nothing Do is built for iPhone. It supports light and dark appearance and respects iOS accessibility settings like Reduce Motion.
